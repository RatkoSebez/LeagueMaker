Tournament

A tournament is divided into successive rounds. The top-ranked competitors in each fixture progress to the next round. As rounds progress, the number of competitors and fixtures decreases. The final round, usually known as the final or cup final, consists of just one fixture; the winner of which is the overall champion.

League

League is a competition consisting of at least three competitors. It comprises a collection of competitors coming together in a scheduled competition for an overall reward or prize. This formation gives a suitable structure that allows competitors to rank their different skill sets and degrees of performance.
